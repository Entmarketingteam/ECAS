## Raw Data Notes & Methodology

- **Congressional data source:** US House of Representatives STOCK Act Periodic Transaction Reports (PTRs), downloaded as PDFs from `disclosures-clerk.house.gov`. Filings for 2025 and 2026 fiscal years.
- **Senate data:** Not available (Senate Financial Disclosure System DNS not resolving from this environment). Report covers House only.
- **13F data source:** SEC EDGAR submissions API + Archives. Filings are Q4 2025 (period ending Dec 31, 2025), filed Feb 2026 per SEC deadline.
- **13F values:** Reported in thousands of USD per SEC standard. Multiple entries per ticker represent different position types (common stock, call options, put options, convertible notes). Totals are aggregate of all position types per security.
- **False positive mitigations:** "D" ticker matches are filtered to exclude Toronto-Dominion Bank, Old Dominion Freight. "SO" filtered to exclude Southern Copper. "ETN" filtered to exclude Eaton Vance (unrelated company). "BA" filtered for context (Barclays ADS matches excluded by requiring "BOEING CO" pattern).
- **Bridgewater:** XML namespace parsing applied. 26 target sector tickers confirmed with clean matching.
- **Viking Global:** 41KB XML, minimal holdings — only BA found at $858M. Consistent with Viking's concentration style.
- **Tiger Global:** 54 total holdings, tech-focused fund. Only GEV in target sectors ($636M).
